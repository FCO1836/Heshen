Our Projects

Our flagship initiative provides subsidized firearm training for women in Israel’s border communities, led by certified instructors.

Participants gain skills in self-defense, confidence, and situational awareness— helping them protect their homes and children in times of crisis.

Additional community programs will be developed to strengthen resilience, education, and Jewish identity for all residents.
