Our Key Initiatives

Our flagship program offers subsidized safety and preparedness training for women in Israel’s border and rural communities, led by certified experts. Participants build self-defense skills, boost confidence, and sharpen situational awareness—equipping them to care for their homes and loved ones under any circumstance.

We’re also restoring and creating a network of biblical lookouts at historic vantage points. These scenic platforms reconnect visitors with our ancestral heritage, offer panoramic views of the land, and foster a deeper appreciation for the places where our traditions first took root.

Each summer we run immersive camps for kids that blend outdoor adventure, hands-on explorations of biblical stories, and traditional arts. Through hikes, storytelling around ancient sites, and creative workshops, children form lasting bonds with the land, one another, and their Jewish identity.

Alongside these flagship projects, we continue developing community programs to strengthen resilience, education, and cultural identity for all residents— building a vibrant, connected network across the region.
