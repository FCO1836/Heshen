About Hoshen

Hoshen - Connection, Roots, Eternity is a nonprofit organization dedicated to enhancing the physical and mental resilience of residents in Israel’s vital border communities.

Founded by committed members of the community, our mission is to empower Jewish women by providing them with practical tools like firearm training, and cultivating strong connections to land, heritage, and shared purpose.

This initiative is inspired by the values of חיבור (Connection), שורשים (Roots), and נצח (Eternity) — a call to strengthen Israel’s home front, its families, and its future.
