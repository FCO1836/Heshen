About Hoshen

“Hoshen – Connection, Roots, Eternity” empowers Israel’s seam-line communities by strengthening their physical and mental resilience. Our flagship project provides subsidized self defense training—led by certified instructors—for the courageous men and women of these border areas.

  • Stronger Connections: Fostering unity and security within the communities.
  • Deepening Roots: Equipping residents to protect their homes and reinforce their bond to the land.
  • Practical Tools: Providing lifelong skills that safeguard families and the community.

Join us in this vital mission. Your support—financial or by spreading the word—helps us launch and expand this important training program.
