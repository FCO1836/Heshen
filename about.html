About Hoshen

“Hoshen – Connection, Roots, Eternity” empowers Israel’s seam-line communities by strengthening their physical and mental resilience. Our flagship project provides subsidized firearm training—led by certified instructors—for the courageous women of these border areas.

  • Stronger Connections: Fostering unity and security within the communities.
  • Deepening Roots: Equipping residents to protect their homes and reinforce their bond to the land.
  • Ensuring Eternity: Providing lifelong skills that safeguard families and the community.

Join us in this vital mission. Your support—financial or by spreading the word— helps us launch and expand this important training program.
