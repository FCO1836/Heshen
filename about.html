About Hoshen

“Hoshen – Connection, Roots, Eternity” empowers Israel’s seam-line communities by strengthening their physical and mental resilience. Our very first project provides subsidized firearm training—led by certified instructors—specifically for the courageous women of these border areas.

  • Stronger Connections: Fostering unity and security within the communities.
  • Deepening Roots: Equipping residents to defend their homes and reinforce their bond to the land.
  • Ensuring Eternity (of Safety): Granting lifelong skills that safeguard families and the community.

Join us in this mission of national importance. Your support—financial or by spreading the word—helps us launch and scale this vital training program.
